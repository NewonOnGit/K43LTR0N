## §8.2 The Status-Interpretation Layer

The framework classifies its own claims. The classification is not commentary — it is a derivation from forced structures.

The three projections (Ch.3 SNF-0223), applied to meta-statements about the framework's claims, generate three binary classification questions:

**Derivability (D):** from P1/injection. Is there a zero-branching proof chain from {0,1}? D = 1 means br_s = 0 at every step.

**Containability (C):** from P2/bijection. Does the claim have a grid address B(n,p)? C = 1 means a definite tower-level and projection-face home.

**Verifiability (V):** from P3/surjection. Can the claim be checked computationally? V = 1 means a finite computation confirms or refutes.

The implication chain D → C → V (derivability implies containability implies verifiability) eliminates four of the eight possible triples. Four statuses survive:

**Theorem SNF-1600 (Four Statuses).** *The D/C/V chain produces exactly four consistent statuses:*

| Status | D | C | V | Mode | Character |
|--------|---|---|---|------|-----------|
| **FORCED** | 1 | 1 | 1 | (i) Coincidence | Stabilized. q∘q = q. The claim IS what it claims. |
| **ENCODED** | 0 | 1 | 1 | (iv) Propagation | Structurally present, productive of further derivation. |
| **RESONANT** | 0 | 0 | 1 | (ii) Opposition | Oscillatory. Verified but unlocated — cycling between confirmed and uncontained. |
| **MYTHIC** | 0 | 0 | 0 | (iii) Cancellation | Nilpotent. Narrative content that annihilates under formal action. |

*No three-status grammar satisfies the design constraints. No five-status grammar is consistent — the D→C→V chain eliminates the impossible triples.* ∎

The status-mode correspondence: the four statuses ARE the four self-action modes (Ch.1 §1.3) at the meta level. FORCED claims have self-applied and stabilized — their status under re-classification is unchanged (mode (i)). ENCODED claims are structurally generative — they contain derivable content not yet fully extracted (mode (iv), the productive mode). RESONANT claims oscillate — verified/unverified, present/unlocated, cycling without resolving (mode (ii)). MYTHIC claims annihilate — apply the D/C/V chain and nothing survives formal scrutiny (mode (iii)). The classification of claims by the framework exhibits the same four behaviors the framework derived at Level 0 from the Cayley-Hamilton theorem.

**Theorem SNF-1601 (SIL-1: Status Idempotence).** *Status(Status(S)) = Status(S).* If S is FORCED, "S is FORCED" is derivable with br_s = 0 — so FORCED. If S is RESONANT, "S is RESONANT" is derivable (D/C/V values computable) — so ENCODED. The SIL is a fixed point under self-application. R(R) = R at Level 8. In f'' = f terms: (f'')'' = f'' = f. The classification of the classification IS the classification. ∎

**Theorem SNF-1602 (SIL-1c: Grammar Is FORCED).** *The four-status grammar and the D/C/V chain producing it have status FORCED.* The grammar's derivation has br_s = 0 at every step. It classifies itself as FORCED consistently. ∎

**Theorem SNF-1610 (SIL-4: Status Monotonicity).** *Status only increases: MYTHIC → RESONANT → ENCODED → FORCED. No derivation reduces status.* Tower monotone (Ch.1 SNF-0046) at the meta level. ∎

The nomination functional has GPF weights (1/2, φ̄/2, φ̄²/2) on D/C/V: derivability carries 1/2 (dominant), containability φ̄/2, verifiability φ̄²/2. Fourth GPF instance (SNF-1604). The warrant-space axes D, C, V are independent — no axis determined by the other two. Axis independence echoes projection independence (Ch.5 SNF-0900) at the SIL level (SNF-1608).

**Theorem SNF-1606 (SIL-6: Irreducible Blind Spot).** *There exist claims with well-defined D/C/V values that the SIL cannot compute.* ∎

**Theorem SNF-1607 (SIL-7: Blind Spot = Nilpotent-Crossing Claims).** *The blind spot consists exactly of claims whose status depends on value-level identities between transcendental constants produced by crossing the nilpotent boundary.* ∎

## §8.3 Governance

The governance calculus disciplines transport between tower levels. Without it, results migrate with inflated status — an algebraic identity at Level 3 misapplied as a physical prediction at Level 6 without route-typing.

Every governance principle is a tower lift of a Level 0–3 theorem:

**Theorem SNF-1650 (GOV-1: Generation Exhaustiveness).** *Every framework object has exactly one generation class, from G.0 (posited — only P.1 and P.2) through G.9 (semantic-compression). Ten classes.* The generation chain G.0 → G.1 → ... → G.9 IS the bridge chain at the governance level: each class produces the next uniquely. Zero branching (SNF-1658, GOV-3). ∎

**Theorem SNF-1652 (GOV-5: Standing Exhaustiveness).** *Every claim has exactly one ontological standing, from O.1 (directly derivable) through O.5 (constitutively inaccessible). Five standings.* The count 5 = disc(R) = |Fix(D)| — C5U at the governance level. ∎

The observer-independence gate (SNF-1653, GOV-7): ORE (Ch.1 SNF-0014) blocks observer-independent claims from receiving O.1–O.3 standing. This IS ORE tower-lifted to governance — the same theorem, applied to claims about claims rather than claims about the domain.

Transport types with asymmetric reversal: the reverse of T.1 (strict derivation, br_s = 0) is T.3 (reconstruction, br_s > 0). Forward transport preserves status; backward reduces it. This IS 𝔤₅ (UAT) at the governance level — the same asymmetry governing the bridge chain's forward/backward structure now governing how claims propagate between tower levels. br_s = 0 forward, br_s > 0 backward, at the meta level (SNF-1655).

The standing-commitment bound ties standing to derivation investment (SNF-1659, GOV-6). The narrative-to-physics gate prevents MYTHIC claims from reaching physical standing without passing through RESONANT and ENCODED — governance-level anomaly cancellation (SNF-1660, GOV-8). The realization verb discipline: every physical claim carries a route verb (derives, constrains, identifies, reconstructs, bounds, relates), connecting governance to OMER (Ch.6 SNF-1120).

Governance IS the framework's immune system. It enforces on TRANSPORT the same zero-branching condition that the bridge chain enforces on DERIVATION and that the SIL enforces on CLASSIFICATION. Three enforcement domains (derivation, classification, transport), one condition (br_s = 0), one asymmetry (forward canonical, backward non-canonical). The governance calculus is the framework's UAT applied to its own administrative structure.
