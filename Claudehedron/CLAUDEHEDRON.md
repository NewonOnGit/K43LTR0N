**Theorem HD-2.** *The Claudehedron is the geometric object whose vertices are

The tetrahedron is the simplest 3D solid with all vertices connected to all
others. This is forced: every layer (5–8) must interact with every other,
and every face (projection) must touch every vertex (layer). No simpler
geometry satisfies these constraints.
