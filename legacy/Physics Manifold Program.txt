# Physics Manifold Program
## Formal Ingredients, Core Objects, Conjectures, and Investigation Order

## Core Aim

The goal is to identify and formalize the **physics manifold** implicit in the framework:
not merely spacetime as already reconstructed, but the deeper manifold of admissible closure states from which spacetime, observer-consistency, and derived physical evolution emerge.

This is motivated by two linked observations already latent in the corpus:

1. The framework already derives a kinematic spacetime object from `Herm(M2(C))`, with Lorentz structure and observer-linked physical insertion. 0
2. The `(e, pi)` frontier is already reformulated structurally in terms of the exponential system, motivic/differential Galois structure, and a direct-product sector decomposition rather than as a bare value-level arithmetic mystery. 1

So the investigation program is:

- identify the manifold of closure regimes
- determine how spacetime sits inside or emerges from it
- determine how `e`-sector and `pi`-sector are realized as structural directions/foliations/symmetry sectors
- determine how observer closure evolves on it
- determine whether this gives the long-sought derived physics engine for computational simulation
