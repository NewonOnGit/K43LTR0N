# PART I: THE META-ARCHITECTURE THESIS

## §1 The Central Discovery

The framework already contains Levels 7 (Meta) and 8 (Semantic). Three projections × two meta-levels = six cells. The investigation document (FUNDAMENTAL_METACATEGORIES_INVESTIGATION) identified six metacategory axes. These are not arbitrary — they ARE the six cells B(7–8, P1–P3).

**Thesis:** The six metacategory axes are the three projections applied at the two meta-levels. The framework's own grid predicts them.

### §1.1 The 2×3 Meta-Grid

|  | **P1 (Production)** | **P2 (Mediation)** | **P3 (Observation)** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 7 — Meta** | **Generation** B(7,P1): How does this come into being? | **Epistemic Status** B(7,P2): What warrants assertion? | **Ontological Standing** B(7,P3): What kind of thing is it? |
| **Level 8 — Semantic** | **Structural Role** B(8,P1): What job does it do? | **Transport Class** B(8,P2): What cross-layer move is legal? | **Semantic Role** B(8,P3): What meaning relation is asserted? |

### §1.2 Why This Assignment

Each cell inherits its projection's character applied to meta-level content:

**B(7,P1) = Generation.** P1 is the productive act. At the meta-level: what productive mechanism generated this object/claim? The P1 character (composition, compression, return) maps to: derivation chain, origin layer, mechanism type, uniqueness. This is where "forced" fractures into its sub-species.

**B(7,P2) = Epistemic Status.** P2 mediates between levels. At the meta-level: what warrants the transit from unasserted to asserted? P2's character (balance, transition, minimality) maps to: the D→C→V chain already in T_SIL, but enriched to a multidimensional warrant space.

**B(7,P3) = Ontological Standing.** P3 observes/discloses. At the meta-level: what kind of existence does this object have when you look at it? P3's character (quotient, kernel, revelation-through-annihilation) maps to: formal vs observer-relative vs physical-candidate vs narrative. The observation determines what kind of thing you're looking at.

**B(8,P1) = Structural Role.** P1 at the semantic level: what productive job does this play in the architecture? Generator, mediator, invariant, obstruction, classifier. The role grammar.

**B(8,P2) = Transport Class.** P2 at the semantic level: what kind of cross-layer movement is legal? P2 mediates transitions; at the semantic level it governs which transitions between domains preserve meaning. The transport legality matrix.

**B(8,P3) = Semantic Role.** P3 at the semantic level: what kind of naming/meaning relation is invoked? Identity, equivalence, encoding, compression, resonance, projection-name. Observation of vocabulary reveals what kind of claim the words are making.

### §1.3 Self-Consistency Check

The 2×3 assignment must satisfy three framework constraints:

**Independence.** No axis should be definable from the other two within its row. Check: Generation (mechanism) ≠ function of Epistemic Status (warrant) and Ontological Standing (existence type). An object can be bridge-forced (Generation) with ENCODED epistemic status and formal ontological standing — each coordinate varies independently. ✓

**Containment.** Each axis should contain recognizable images of the others (Folding Theorem at meta-level). Check: Generation contains epistemic information (derivation implies warrant). Epistemic Status contains generation information (the D-question asks about derivability). Ontological Standing contains both (existence type constrains what generation mechanisms are available and what warrants are admissible). ✓ — containment without definability.

**Central Collapse.** The three axes at each row should exhaust the meta-content at that level. Check: For any framework object at Level 7, asking how it was generated (P1), what warrants it (P2), and what kind of thing it is (P3) should leave no meta-question unanswered at that level. ✓ — these three questions are P1∘P2∘P3 = Dist at the meta-level.

### §5.4 The Physics Smuggling Detector

A cross-layer move is **physics smuggling** when it:
1. Imports a physical concept (mass, charge, field, force) as a derivational step rather than as a derivational output
2. Uses transport class T.9 (interpretive) while claiming T.1 (strict derivation)
3. Transits from D.6→D.2 (forces→algebra) without going through D.4 (observer layer)
4. Assigns standing O.6 (physical interpretation) to an object with generation class G.9 (semantic) or G.10 (bookkeeping)

**The P3 criterion (T_SIL §4):** No concept import. Standard math as language is permitted; physical concepts as derivational steps are prohibited. T_TRANS makes this precise: the only legal path to D.5/D.6 (physics) runs through D.4 (observer), and the transport must be T.1, T.2, or T.6.
